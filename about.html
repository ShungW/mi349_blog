About Me

Hi, my name is Shungloon Wong, but I just go by Shung. I am currently studying Information Science at MSU, and I hope to be graduating by next year. Most of my hobbies are indoors, besides cycling, which I do recreationally or on my commute to campus.

My other hobbies are mostly indoors, which include items like:

  • Mechanical Keyboards
  • Headphones/Audio
  • Records (I just like the experience)
  • 3-D printing/Modeling

I consider myself to be rather adept at soldering, I like making cables for headphones or other peripherals, and I enjoy coming up with different color combinations for them. I have also dabbled in modeling and 3-D printing keyboard cases, though in comparison to others in the hobby, I have barely touched the surface.
